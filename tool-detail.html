Gemini for Gmail — Quick Overview

3 minutes · Updated March 2026 · 12,400 views
Step-by-Step
How to get started
~5 minutes total

You don't need to install anything new — Gemini is already built into Gmail. You just need to know where to find it and how to turn it on. Follow these steps and you'll be writing AI-assisted emails in under 5 minutes.

✓
Open Gmail in your browser
Go to gmail.com and sign in with your Google account. Gemini works in the browser version — not just the mobile app.
30 sec
2
Click "Compose" to write a new email
Hit the blue Compose button in the top left. A new email window will open at the bottom right of your screen.
10 sec
3
Find the Gemini pencil icon
Look at the bottom of the compose window. You'll see a small pencil icon with a star — that's Gemini. Click it to open the AI writing panel.
30 sec
4
Describe what you want to write
Type a short description of your email in plain language. Example: "Write a friendly follow-up email to a client I met last week at a conference." Gemini will draft the full email for you.
1 min
5
Review, edit, and send
Gemini inserts the draft into your compose window. Read it over, make any tweaks you want, then send. You can also click "Refine" to ask Gemini to adjust the tone or length.
2 min
💡
Pro tip: The more detail you give Gemini, the better the result. Instead of "write a follow-up email," try "write a friendly but professional follow-up to Sarah at Acme Co. about our proposal from last Thursday." Specific prompts get specific, usable emails.
